Viz-A-Thon: The Art of Visual Data

Greetings from the FODSE CLUB✨

Transform raw data into stunning insights! Showcase your data skills in this two-round competition! Clean, analyze, and visualize real-world datasets using Power BI, Tableau, Python and more. Let your creativity and analytical thinking shine! Analyze. Visualize. Win! Team Size: 2 members

Rules: Round 1 – Data Cleaning Participants will refine raw datasets by handling duplicates and missing values, ensuring the data is structured, accurate, and ready for analysis.

Round 2 – Data Visualization Teams will craft impactful visualisations using Visualization Tools, selecting key data points to convey meaningful insights while also evaluating and improving their designs. Participants will present their in-depth analysis and findings. Their work will be evaluated by a panel of judges on creativity, presentation, and their ability to address questions raised by the judges.

UNITED NATIONS DAY ML CHALLENGE

Greetings from the FODSE CLUB✨

This event offers a hands-on opportunity to develop machine learning models focused on real-world issues aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Participants will engage with a provided dataset to predict outcomes related to critical topics such as climate action, health, and clean energy.

The challenge emphasizes practical applications of machine learning techniques, equipping participants with the skills and knowledge needed to address current global challenges. This immersive experience combines practical exercises with impactful objectives, ensuring that learners are ready to tackle the demands of today’s data-driven landscape.

WORKSHOP ON ESSENTIAL AZURE

Greetings from the FODSE CLUB✨

This workshop provides an extensive overview to the fundamental Azure services used in data engineering. Participants acquire hands-on experience developing, coordinating, and monitoring data pipelines, transforming data using Databricks and Spark, and doing sophisticated data analysis with Azure Synapse Analytics.

The session focuses on integrating Azure's data services to manage complex data operations, equipping students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in Azure data engineering. It integrates hands-on exercises with real-world scenarios to guarantee that learners are prepared for the challenges of current data engineering work.

Ease The Error 4.0

Greetings from the FODSE CLUB✨

Ease the Error 4.0 was a 24-hour inter-college Hackathon conducted on the 6th and 7th of April 2024 with the combined effort of the Forum of Data Science Engineers (FODSE) and The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers – Student’s Forum (IETE).

During the entire event, the mentors and alumni periodically surveyed the progress of all the teams and also provided technical knowledge and mentoring when required. The teams pushed themselves to innovate new models and solutions for their problem statements. Food and refreshments were provides with cash prizes and certificates.

The Mentorship Programme at the Forum Of Data Science Engineers (FODSE) aimed to introduce beginners to Data Science and Python programming. VIZ-A-THON

Greetings from the FODSE CLUB✨

"Viz-A-Thon: The Art of Visual Data" was a dynamic data visualization challenge held on October 27th, 2023. The event comprised two rounds focusing on data cleaning and visualization, fostering collaboration among participants.

The event aimed to familiarize students with modern visualization tools and techniques while addressing key program outcomes such as problem analysis and teamwork. Participants engaged in practical data analysis and visualization tasks,culminating in a comprehensive judging process led by a diverse panel. The event concluded with winners announced on social media, followed by a heartfelt vote of thanks.

INAUGURAL’23

Greetings from the FODSE CLUB✨

The Forum of Data Science Engineers (FODSE) inauguration at SVCE, organized by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering in collaboration with the Department of Information Technology, was a meticulously orchestrated event held on September 20, 2023.

The ceremony reached its pinnacle with the distribution of badges and certificates, recognizing the dedication and talent of the newly appointed office bearers and mentors. This event not only inspired students but also highlighted the transformative potential of data science in fostering innovation and driving positive social change.

Scraper's Soiree

Greetings from the FODSE CLUB✨

The Forum of Datascience Engineers (FODSE) organized the "Scraper's Soiree," a web scraping event focused on automating data extraction from websites using code. This event allowed participants to enhance their skills in retrieving and analyzing data for various applications. It offered a hands-on experience in harnessing web scraping techniques, enabling students to explore real-world applications of data collection and analysis through automation. The winning team, TrueDamage, consisted of VigneshRaju who tackled the problem statement 'Machine Learning'. The runner-up team, TechTitans, included Robinkumar and Magesh who tackled the problem statement in 'Artificial Intelligence'. A special mention was given to LSK which included Kiran Sekar and LathikaaShri who worked on 'Web Development'.
