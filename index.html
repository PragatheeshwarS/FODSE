Who We Are

The Forum of Data Science Engineers (FODSE) is a highly premium club of SVCE founded on the 6th of December 2020. It was formed by the collaboration of IETE and AIT, SVCE. FODSE is devoted for the students who are passionate towards Data Science, Data Analytics, ML, AI and Statistics. We encourage our student members to build their skills and develop projects in the same given proper guidance from our forum mentors.

Our forum conducts webinars ,guest lectures ,technical events (such as hackathons , workshops , quizzes , case studies , etc.) frequently. Our goal is to turn data into information and information to insight.

So...Why FODSE?

Important career field

☛ Data Science is a relatively new and exciting field offering a wide scope of opportunities when it comes to career

Potential job growth

☛ Some of the students who have been working in the field and could realize its potential are keen to develop a culture where the interested students can discuss this vast field under one umbrella

Teach students

☛ We started this club to elevate the benchmark of work that has been happening in our college and make more and more people aware of the data science in general

Student community

☛ We strive to be the single focal point of overall development and growth of the student community with interests in areas of data analytics, machine learning, and big data
