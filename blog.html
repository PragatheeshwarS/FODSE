Fodse Inauguration

FODSE
Published on Aug 4, 2024

The Forum of Data Science Engineers (FODSE) club at Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering (SVCE) is set to hold its inaugural event on September 3rd week, 2023. The event will mark the launch of the club for the Acadmeic year 2023-24 and introduce its members to the college community.

FODSE is a student-led club aimed at promoting awareness and understanding of data science and its applications. The club's activities include workshops, seminars, and projects related to data science and engineering. Through these activities, FODSE hopes to equip its members with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the field of data science.

The inauguration event will feature speeches from the club's founding members, as well as from distinguished guests in the field of data science. The event will also include interactive sessions and activities for attendees to learn more about the club's goals and activities.

The event is open to all SVCE students who are interested in data science and engineering.
